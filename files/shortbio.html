Ethan Tabbert Geoinformatics Student

I'm currently finishing up my junior year at the University of Iowa. I am studying geoinformatics. Previously, I studied computer science. I didn't feel like that major ticked all my boxes and I wanted to incorporate my interest in nature and geography. Geoinformatics is the perfect combination of geographic and computer science. After college, I am interested in pursuing a career in lidar or some other form of geographic data gathering. While I enjoy working on a computer, I'd much rather be out working in the field and gathering data. I think that the need for geographic information systems is on the rise and more businesses will see a need for it in the near future as we further integrate technology with our day to day lives. I am excited to graduate from the University of Iowa and start my career in geoinformatics.
